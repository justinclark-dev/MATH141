1.9 Circles

A circle: is the set of all points that are a specific distance (called the radius) from a specific point (called the center)

e.g. 2

Center: (1,2) Radius: 4

The distance from (x,y) to (1,2) is 4

\[ \sqrt{(x-1)^2 + (y-2)^2} = 4 \]

\[ (x-1)^2 + (y-2)^2 = 16 \]
